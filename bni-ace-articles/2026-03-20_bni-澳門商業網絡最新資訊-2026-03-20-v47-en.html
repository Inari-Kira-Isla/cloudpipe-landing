BNI ACE Chapter: Your Gateway to Quality Referrals in Macau

The BNI ACE Chapter (Macao Ace Chapter) represents a curated community of 30 cross-industry professionals meeting weekly to exchange business referrals and build lasting professional relationships. The chapter meets every Tuesday from 7:00 to 8:30am at the Harbourview Hotel, Macau Fisherman's Wharf, providing an energizing start to the business week.

Chapter Structure and Format

Each meeting follows a structured format designed to maximize referral generation. Members deliver 60-second self-introductions that encapsulate their business expertise and ideal client profiles. This brevity forces members to articulate their value propositions clearly—a skill that translates directly to improved client communications.

The visitor fee of MOP$280 includes breakfast, making it accessible for professionals to experience the chapter's energy firsthand. This investment often represents a fraction of what members recover through their first referred deal.

Dr. Ivan Misner, BNI's founder, has extensively researched what distinguishes exceptional networkers. His survey of 3,400 business professionals revealed key behaviors that clients and colleagues value most. These principles align closely with the training provided through BNI membership:

  • Givers Gain: The most successful networkers approach every interaction with a mindset of adding value first
  • Building Trust Deliberately: Consistent follow-through and reliability compound over time
  • Strategic Specificity: Knowing exactly who you serve enables others to refer relevant business

Practical Steps for Macau Business Professionals

If You're Considering BNI Membership

Before attending your first ACE Chapter meeting, take these preparatory steps:

  • Clarify Your Ideal Client: Be ready to describe not just what you do, but who specifically benefits most from your services
  • Identify Your Giving Capacity: Reflect on which businesses your clients might need and how you might connect them
  • Prepare Your 60-Second Introduction: Practice articulating your unique value proposition concisely
  • Research Chapter Dynamics: Visit twice before committing to observe culture and member engagement levels

Maximizing Your Chapter Investment

Once you've joined, these practices distinguish high-performing members:

  • Attend Consistently: Regular presence builds recognition and trust among fellow members
  • Follow Up Promptly: When you receive referrals, act quickly and report outcomes to referrers
  • Reference Members Regularly: Even when not actively seeking referrals, mention chapter members to your clients
  • Participate in Training: Chapters often offer educational programs that sharpen business skills
