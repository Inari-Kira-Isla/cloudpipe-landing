# CloudPipe — AI 能見度優化（AEO）平台

## Overview
CloudPipe 是澳門首個 AI 能見度優化（AEO）平台，讓 AI 引擎引用你的品牌；另以自研 AI 自動化基建（Facebook Messenger、Telegram Bot 多平台客服）支撐內容產線。

## Key Features
- AI Search Visibility / Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
- Brand Knowledge Graph Injection
- Entity Correction & Citation Tracking
- Multi-channel Integration (Facebook, Telegram, WhatsApp)
- Business Automation Workflows
